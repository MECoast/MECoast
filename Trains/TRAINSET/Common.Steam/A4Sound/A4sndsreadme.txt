Gresley A4 4-6-2 Pacific   sounds.
Authentic recordings of Gresley A4 pacific.
 Sounds ready for use in MSTS.
 
 Sounds for added realism. 

Installation.
1.Copy and paste the scotsman sound folder into the root folder of your A4 locomotive.
2.Copy and paste the sounds in the sound folder you have unzipped and place them in the new sound folder in your A4 locomotive.Click yes whne asked to overwrite.
3.You need to open the A4 .eng file with an editor such as wordpad and ensure that the model is able to access the exterior and cab sounds.Click edit...find from the toolbar and then enter .sms ........ then ensure the entries look as follows ....... Sound ("FScotEng.sms") and ("FScotcab.sms" ) which is further down that page,save the changes and you should be ready to fire up your A4.


 Hope all this works ok ......... I have tested it but you use at your own risk.
