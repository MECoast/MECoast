A Bogie van to match the Light railway coaches.

Three vans are included.
Two vacuum brakes vans in blue and varnished wood and a red air braked van for the export market.

The repainters kit insructions included with the LR_Bogie_Coach also apply to these vans
The instructions also detail how to rename the ace files and edit the reference in the shape file.

Note that the air and vacuum braked vans use different shape files but the repainting instructions and ace files are common to both.


INSTALLATION
~~~~~~~~~~~~

Open the zip file and extract to a temporary folder. Drag Copy or Move the LR_Bogie_Van folder to you MSTS trainset folder.
You will have to make a consist to see these coaches in MSTS.
