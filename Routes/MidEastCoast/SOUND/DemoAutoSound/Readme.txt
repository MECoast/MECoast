Demo sound files for Open Rails automatic track sound 

This package includes demo sound files to test the Open Rails automatic track sound on curves with small radius and switches.
It includes a switch sound, a curve squeal sound and a curve/switch sound.

The package must be unpacked into the main Train Simulator Sound folder, and creates a DemoAutoSound subfolder.

The package is intended only for personal use and can't be uploaded or sent to other people or sold without written permission.
